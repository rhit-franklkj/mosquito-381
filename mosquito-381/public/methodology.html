Methodology

After a cursory overview of the five solutions presented, a bit of research was performed on each to determine if there would be any negative anthropological or ecological consequences.

Once those lists were compiled, we turned our attention to the PDF graphs in order to determine if any particular one held advantages over any of the others by way of asymptotic analysis.

The cost of each of these methods was then considered. Solutions that cost more to achieve the same confidence interval with similar outside consequences would be penalized for that.
